How to install — 3 steps

  1. 1Open the .dmg — double-click it in your Downloads folder. A window will appear with the app icon.
  2. 2Drag to Applications — drag Sway into the Applications shortcut in that window.
  3. 3Approve the app — macOS may say it can't verify the developer. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click "Open Anyway". Only needed once.
  4. 4Sign in — use your work email and the password you just set on this page.
